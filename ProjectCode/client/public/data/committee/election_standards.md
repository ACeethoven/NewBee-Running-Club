# Committee Election Standards
# 委员会选举/换届标准

## General Requirements
## 基本要求

1. **Membership Duration**
   - Must be a club member for at least 1 year
   - Active participation in club activities
   - Regular attendance at training sessions

2. **Leadership Qualities**
   - Demonstrated leadership skills
   - Good communication abilities
   - Team player mentality
   - Problem-solving capabilities

3. **Commitment**
   - Available for regular committee meetings
   - Willing to dedicate time for club activities
   - Long-term commitment to club development

## Election Process
## 选举流程

1. **Nomination Phase**
   - Self-nomination or member nomination
   - Submission of candidate statement
   - Review by current committee

2. **Voting Process**
   - All club members eligible to vote
   - Secret ballot system
   - Simple majority required

3. **Term Duration**
   - 2-year term for all positions
   - Maximum 2 consecutive terms
   - Re-election possible after 1 term break

## Position-Specific Requirements
## 职位具体要求

### President
- Minimum 2 years of club membership
- Previous committee experience preferred
- Strong organizational skills
- Vision for club development

### Board Members
- Minimum 1 year of club membership
- Active participation in club activities
- Specific skills relevant to position
- Commitment to club values

## Transition Process
## 换届流程

1. **Handover Period**
   - 1-month transition period
   - Documentation of responsibilities
   - Training of new committee members

2. **Knowledge Transfer**
   - Detailed handover documents
   - Shadowing period
   - Regular meetings between outgoing and incoming members

3. **Evaluation**
   - Performance review of outgoing committee
   - Feedback collection from members
   - Implementation of improvements
